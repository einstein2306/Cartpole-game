Cart Pole

This environment is part of the classic control environments. Here the goal is to keep the Pole upright position by moving the cart left and right. It need some parameters to consider. They belongs to Cart and Pole. Those are the (4,) observations and (2,) actions.

Parameter Parameter Data
Action Space Discrete(2)
Observation Shape (4,)
Action High [4.8 inf 0.42 inf]
Observation Low [-4.8 -inf -0.42 -inf]
Import gym.make("CartPole-v1")

Action Space

The action space is ndarry with shape (1,) which can take values {0, 1} indicating the direction of the fixed force the cart is pushed with.

Number Action
0 Push cart to the left
1 Push cart to the right

Observation Space

The observation is a ndarry with shape (4,) with the values corresponding to the following postions and velocities.

Num Observation Min Max
0 Cart Position -4.8 4.8
1 Cart Velocity -inf inf
2 Pole Angle ~ -0.418
rad(-24°)		 ~ 0.418
rad(24°)
3 Pole Angular Velocity -inf inf

Rewards

Since the goal is to keep the pole upright Position for as long as possible, a reward of +1 for every step taken, including the termination step, is allotted. The threshold for rewards is 475 for v1.

Note

The above all parameters and measurements belongs to gym environment which we used in AIMode to train the CartPole Agent.
